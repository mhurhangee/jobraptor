# What is JobRaptor?

JobRaptor is a powerful job search platform designed to help job seekers find their ideal positions quickly and efficiently. Our platform uses advanced algorithms to match candidates with the most relevant job opportunities based on their skills, experience, and preferences.

## Key Features

- **Smart Job Matching**: Our AI-powered system analyzes your profile to find the most suitable job openings.
- **Real-time Alerts**: Get notified immediately when relevant positions become available.
- **Application Tracking**: Keep track of all your applications in one place.
- **Interview Preparation**: Access resources to help you prepare for interviews.

## How It Works

1. Create your profile and upload your resume
2. Set your job preferences and desired location
3. Review matched job opportunities
4. Apply directly through our platform
5. Track your application status

JobRaptor is committed to simplifying the job search process and helping you land your dream job with minimal stress and maximum efficiency.
